Tic Tac Toe

  1. Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. Players take turns marking empty squares with their symbol, either "X" or "O".
  3. The objective is to create a row of three of your symbols either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  4. The game ends when one player achieves a winning combination or when all squares are filled without a winner.
  5. Players cannot overwrite or change their marks once placed.
  6. The first player to create a winning row wins the game.
  7. No cheating is allowed during the game.
